Martin Garrix Shines at AMF 2024, Clinches DJ Mag's #1 Spot Again

By Rodel Sumalinog | Published on 11/20/24

Amsterdam Music Festival (AMF) 2024 witnessed an unforgettable performance by the one and only Martin Garrix, who once again proved why he reigns supreme in the electronic dance music world. The Dutch sensation delivered a high-energy live set packed with his iconic hits, new festival bangers, and breathtaking visuals that left the crowd electrified.

Adding to the celebration, Martin Garrix was officially crowned the #1 DJ in the world by DJ Mag's prestigious Top 100 DJs poll for 2024. This marks yet another milestone in his illustrious career, solidifying his place as a global icon in dance music.

Martin Garrix's rise to the top has been fueled by his relentless dedication, innovation, and connection with fans worldwide. His UMF 2024 performance and DJ Mag accolade are testaments to his enduring influence and unmatched talent in the EDM scene."This is for my fans. You are the reason I do what I do. Thank you for your endless support!" Garrix said during his set, a heartfelt moment that resonated deeply with the crowd. With AMF 2024 setting the tone for another groundbreaking year, it’s clear that Martin Garrix is not just making music—he’s creating history.
